You're in!

Check your email — your child's first StoryTrail adventure is already waiting for you. Here's how to make sure it lands right where you'll see it.

Three quick things

1

Find it, then move it to your main inbox

If it lands in Promotions or another tab, drag it into your Primary inbox.

2

Star it

One tap tells your inbox this is something you actually want to see.

3

Reply with anything

Even just "got it!" helps a lot. A quick reply is one of the strongest signals we're someone worth hearing from.

Why this matters: we're a brand-new sender, and email providers are cautious with anyone new. Moving, starring, and replying to that first email helps teach inboxes everywhere that StoryTrail is welcome — so your story (and everyone else's) shows up reliably every night, not buried in a folder no one checks.
