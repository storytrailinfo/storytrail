Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

StoryTrail ("we," "us," or "our") provides personalized bedtime stories delivered by email. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card number). We also collect information about your child, such as their first name, age, and stated interests, which is used solely to personalize their stories.

How we use your information

  • To generate and deliver personalized bedtime stories
  • To process your subscription and billing
  • To send account-related emails, such as billing notices or service updates
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve the quality and safety of the stories we generate

How we share your information

We do not sell your information or your child's information to anyone. We share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate StoryTrail, including:

  • Stripe, for payment processing
  • Our email delivery provider, to send your stories
  • Our AI provider, to generate story content based on your child's profile

These providers are only given the information necessary to perform their function and are not permitted to use it for any other purpose.

Children's privacy

StoryTrail is designed for parents and guardians to use on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children, and all child profile information is provided and managed by the parent or guardian's account.

Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, we retain your data for a reasonable period in case you wish to reactivate, after which it may be deleted upon request.

Your choices

You can update your child's profile information at any time using the link included in every email. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account management page. To request deletion of your data, email us at storytrailinfo@gmail.com.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and secure third-party processors for payment and data storage. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information to the best of our ability.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll notify you by email or through a notice on our site.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled, email us at storytrailinfo@gmail.com.
