Mia and the Sky-Blue Dragon

The night Mia climbed to the highest branch of the Whispering Willow, she was not expecting to find a dragon waiting there — a small one, no bigger than her favorite stuffed dinosaur, with scales the exact shade of the sky just after sunset. Its name, she would soon discover, was Cobalt, and it had traveled fourteen thousand leagues to find a girl brave enough to help it carry home a star that had fallen asleep on the wrong cloud…
