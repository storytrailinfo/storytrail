Questions

Good to know

How does this work?
You tell us your child's name, age, and a few things they love — their favorite color, animal, whatever they're into right now. Every night, we write a brand-new bedtime story starring them, built around those details, and email it straight to you before bedtime.
Is the content safe for kids?
Yes. Every story is written specifically for bedtime — gentle, calm, age-appropriate, with no scary content, no violence, and nothing meant to overstimulate right before sleep.
Can I update my child's favorites later?
Anytime. Kids' interests change fast — every email includes a link to update their favorite color, animal, or anything else in seconds. Tomorrow's story will reflect it.
What if I don't like a story?
Every email has a "get a new one" link. You can regenerate a fresh story up to 3 times a day, no questions asked.
What if I have more than one kid?
Our family plan covers up to 4 children for one flat $19/month — each child gets their own story, written just for them, every night.
What if the email doesn't show up?
First, check your spam or promotions folder — we're a new sender, so some inboxes are extra cautious at first. Replying to one of our emails helps a lot. Still nothing? Just email us at storytrailinfo@gmail.com and we'll sort it out.
How does the free trial work, and what happens after?
Your first 3 stories are completely free — no charge up front. After that, your card is billed automatically at $9/month (1 child) or $19/month (2-4 children), unless you cancel before then.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, anytime, no phone calls or emails required. Every email includes a link to manage your account, where you can cancel in a couple clicks.

Why I built this

Bedtime was the best part of my day. I wanted to make it that way for everyone.

When my son was four, I started making up bedtime stories for him — ones where he was the hero, where his favorite dinosaur showed up, where things turned out okay. He'd fall asleep mid-story, and I'd feel like the best dad in the world.

What I didn't expect was how much I loved those 15 minutes. No screens, no noise, just his imagination and mine meeting somewhere in the dark. I wanted every family to have that — not just the ones lucky enough to have a parent who happens to enjoy spinning yarns at 8pm.

So I built StoryTrail. A new story every night, written just for your child, delivered before bedtime. You read. They drift off. That's it.

— Kevin, founder
